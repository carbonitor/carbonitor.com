Our Project

Monitoring and Preventing Corruption in Carbon Offset Project

Public Information Transparency

Shedding Light on the Intersection of Corruption and Information Transparency

Occasionally, the foundation of corruption coincides with the concept of information transparency. Our function is to furnish the public with information that complies with public policies.

Accountability Monitoring System

Ensuring Accuracy and Transparency in Carbon Offset Schemes

We verify, authenticate, and align with publicly available information on the carbon offset scheme. We furnish adequate information to enable the disclosure of public details regarding the activities.

Empowering Through Awareness

Unveiling Illicit Conduct in Carbon Offset Practices

Our monitoring mechanisms resulted in an alert or flag of suspicious activity. Engaging in the opaque practice of divulging public information can generate suspicion and frequently result in illicit behaviour. We deter criminal activities associated with carbon offset and provide education to relevant parties.
